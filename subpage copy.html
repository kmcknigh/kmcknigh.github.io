Welcome

Chapter 1: Grandma's House

"The Blacker the Berry" is an imagined digital safe-space for Black people built by the artist, Khalil McKnight. The space contains an amalgamation of different painted objects from the artist's childhood home; accompanied with motifs inspired by other Black artists who have worked effortlessly to create spaces alike. This space immortalizes memories and underscores the existance of the things we fail to see. Thus, let your eyes adjust to the darkness and enjoy some music as you explore.
